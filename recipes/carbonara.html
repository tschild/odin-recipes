Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This traditional italian Spaghetti dish is always a delicious choice. With only five ingredients (pasta, eggs, pecorino, black pepper and carbonara), Spaghetti Carbonara really is an al time less is more f(l)avourite.

Ingredients

  • 100 gr Carbonara
  • 200 gr Spaghettoni/Spaghetti
  • 50 gr Pecorino Cheese
  • 4 Eggs (yolks)
  • 5 gr Black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the carbonara in bite sized cubes
  2. Fry the choped carbonara pieces on a high temperature, till the outside gets crispy brown. Get some of the fatt out of the pan for later
  3. Boil water, and add the all dried Spagettoni/Spaghetti to it (10-11 min). Continue with next step.
  4. Meanwhile, seperate the 4 egg yolks from the egg whites
  5. Raps the 30gr Peccorino with the 4 egg yolks, and mix it togeheter
  6. When the Spagettoni/Spagetti is ready, save a cup of boiled water and put the pasta in the frying pan
  7. Au bain-marie the frying pan, while adding the yolk/pecorino mix to it, add the cup of boiling water and the carbonara
  8. Mix the frying pan and all the ingredients till slowly, the sauce is thickened til perfection
  9. Dinner can be served, rasp the remaining pecorino on top of the pasta plates, add the black pepper and enjoy!
