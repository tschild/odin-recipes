Spagetti Tomato Summer

Description

This vegetarian Tomato summer Spaghetti dish gives a fresh juicy variant on the common Spaghetti dishes. With only six ingredients (pasta, tomato, parmesan, black olives, basil and garlic), This Tomato Summer Spaghetti really is an refreshing taste on a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • 1 truss Tasty Tom Tomato's
  • 200 gr Spaghettoni/Spaghetti
  • 50 gr Black Olives
  • 1 handfull of fresh Basil
  • 10 gr Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Boil water, add a bit of salt and add the pasta when the water is boiling
  2. Chop the Tomato's in little pieces (dont spill the juice, it gives the freshness!), together with the black olives, the fresh basil and the gloves of garlic
  3. When the Spagettoni/Spaghetti is ready, save a cup of boiled water
  4. Put everything together and mix up all the ingredients, also add the cup of boiled water
  5. Grab some nice plates and rasp the Parmesan cheese on top, as dinner can be served! Hmm the taste of freshness
