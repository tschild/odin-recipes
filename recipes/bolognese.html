Spagetti Bolognese

Description

This richly filled Spaghetti Bolognese dish is a good meal after a hard days work. With over 10 ingredients (pasta, tomato, tomato sauce, zucchini, red paprika, mushrooms, parmesan, basil, minced meat and garlic), This Spaghetti Bolognese has a lot of flavour and energy to share.

Ingredients

  • 1 truss Tasty Tom Tomato's
  • 1 jar Tomato sauce
  • 200 gr Spaghettoni/Spaghetti
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 1 handfull of fresh Basil
  • 10 gr Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 200 gr Mushrooms
  • 1 Red Paprika
  • 200 gr Minced Meat

Steps

  1. Boil water, add a bit of salt and add the pasta when the water is boiling.
  2. Chop the Zucchini, the Mushrooms, the red paprika and the tomato's in bite sized blocks. Shop the garlic in small pieces.
  3. Fry and stir the Minced meat till all pieces are brown. Add the Zuccini and the mushrooms and fry them 7 min.
  4. Add the garlic, tomato's and the red paprika pieces, and after 2 minutes add the tomato sauce.
  5. When the pasta is ready, get rid of the water and add the cooked pasta to the sauce mix
  6. Dinner is ready! Serve out the pasta with a bit of parmesan on top and you have a lovely meal!
